So, what can Chime do?

Wakes you up at your stop

Chime's main function is to wake you up when you reach your stop and that's our top priority, regardless of connection.

Customize your alarm

Here at Chime, we are all about user experience. That's why you can customize how your alarm wakes you up.

Save custom locations

With Chime, you're able to save locations that you visit often. You can save locations as School or Work for ease!

Works for both Trains and Buses

Chime will be fully integrated with the NYC MTA system so it supports both train and bus stops.
