About Chime

Making the next big thing is not about getting rich; it’s about creating something that will solve day to day problems of people. For NYC, our day to day problem is the MTA.

Often unreliable and prone to delays, our city’s public transportation has a reputation for being the number one reason to make you late. To phase out all of the mishaps of delays and construction people from all over the 5 boroughs take naps on the bus or train, but tend to miss their stop. Chime solves this problem.
